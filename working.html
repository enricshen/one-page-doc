Public Feature Service

LINZ_NZ_Property_Titles Last updated: 2022-07-25

Public Feature Service

Provides title information (excluding ownership) where there is a relationship to one or more primary parcels. A Record of Title is a record of a property's owners, legal description and the rights and responsibilities registered against the title.

Credits (Attribution): Land Information New Zealand

LINZ_NZ_Roads_Addressing Last updated: 2022-07-26

Public Feature Service

This layer provides linear geometries against which official road names and street addresses can be recorded. Its purpose is also to enable automated meshblock address reports (for electoral and statistical purposes) so as to identify the presence of a road name in meshblocks where street addresses do not exist.

Credits (Attribution): Land Information New Zealand
